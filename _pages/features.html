Features

Engineered for independence: a stair-climbing, terrain-tackling, restroom-ready robotic wheelchair powered by intelligent, foldable robotics.

Core Capabilities

More than a wheelchair. A robust robotic system.

  • India’s first stair-climbing + restroom-accessible wheelchair
  • Traverses unstructured stairs and rough terrain
  • Four-legged motion system for dynamic balancing
  • Folds to fit in car trunks for on-the-go use
  • Powered by Jetson Nano + ROS2 for semi-autonomy
  • No human assistance required—for restroom, stairs, or cars

Built by a team of passionate young engineers, our system blends cutting-edge AI, mobility mechanics, and human-first design.
